Enrollment Information

Enrollment is limited to the first 104 registered and fully paid campers. We historically reach full enfollment early. Registration includes full payment which is due no later than 24 MAY 2020. Late registration begins June 1st through the first day of camp and incurs a late fee of $15.00 per camper and is based upon enrollment availability. Campers are not registered until full payment is received. For working parents who deduct summer camp as a child care expense our Federal Tax ID number is 45-1612768.
